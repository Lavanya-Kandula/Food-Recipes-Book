Kaju Katli

Kaju katli recipe is actually a royal Indian dessert which takes a delight place in the majority of Indian celebration.

Ingredients

  • Cashew nut - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Ghee - 1 tablespoon
  • Water - as required

Preparation

  1. Take the mixie jar and transfer cashew nut and blend it into smooth powder.
  2. Take a pan and add sugar, little water and boil it to make one and half string sugar syrup consistency.
  3. To it add cashew nut powder and switch off the flame later add 1 teaspoon ghee and mix thoroughly to make the dough.
  4. Grease surface of aplate with little ghee and rolling pin with ghee and roll the dough into Â½ inch thickness.
  5. Leave it for 5 minutes and cut into diamond shapes.
  6. Now the sweet kaju katli is ready to serve.
