Rose Milk

Rose milk is a popular summer beverage made with milk, rose syrup and ice cream. You can easily buy readymade rose syrup in super markets. It is a combination of sugar, water, rose extract and artificial food colouring boiled until syrupy.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Rose Extract
  • Pink Food Color
  • Rose Syrup
  • Ice Cream
  • Ice Cubes

Preparation

  1. For making rose milk, first step you need to make rose syrup. You can buy rose syrup in stores but making it yourselves is super easy. First take sugar in a sauce pan.
  2. Add in water,Heat sugar and water until it gets one string consistency. When you dip the spoon in the syrup and lift it, the last drop falling from the spoon should resembles a string like. It will take around 4 to 5 mins of boiling.
  3. Now take it off the heat and add in rose extract.
  4. Add in pink food colour. If you are not into using artificial food colours, natural food colours are available in the market. You can use that.
  5. Let the syrup cool to room temperature and store in a clean bottle. Your homemade rose syrup is done.
  6. Now lets make rose milkshake. Take cold milk in a blender jar.
  7. Pour in rose syrup.
  8. Blend till creamy.
  9. Take a serving glass Pour shake in and top with icecream and ice cubes.
  10. Now rose Milk is ready to serve.
