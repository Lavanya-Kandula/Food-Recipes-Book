Caramel Milkshake

Delicious caramel milk shake which is so tasty is perfect for this summer time. This caramel milk shake makes a yummy dessert too.

Ingredients

    FOR MILK SHAKE:

  • Frozen Milk - 2 cups
  • Caramel Sauce - as needed
  • Ice Cream as needed

    • FOR CARAMEL:

  • Sugar - ½ cup
  • Cream - ¼ cup
  • Salt - ½ tsp
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp

Preparation

  1. Take sugar in a sauce pan and cook till it gets golden brown.
  2. Add in cream and whisk till creamy.
  3. Add salt, vanilla and mix well. Set aside to cool completely.
  4. Take all ingredients given for milk shake in a blender and puree till smooth.
  5. Take a serving glass, drizzle caramel sauce around the sides of the glass. Pour shake in and top with icecream.
  6. Now Caramel Milkshake is ready to serve.
