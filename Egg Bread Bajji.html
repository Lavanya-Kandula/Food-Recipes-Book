Egg Bread Bajji

Onion masala and boiled egg is used as stuffing between two slices of bread; and this sandwich is dipped into gram flour (Besan) batter and fried. This is really a simple and very enjoyable tea-time snack.

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil and fry till the onion become soft.
  2. Add chopped Capsicum and Green chilli and fry.
  3. Add the spices and fry. Add a little water to prevent the masala from getting burnt.
  4. Take the vessel off from stove after adding chopped Coriander leaves and tomato sauce.
  5. Cut the edges off from the slices carefully.
  6. Smear the cooked masala over the slices. Put half a slice of boiled egg on one of the slices.
  7. Cover it with a second slice and press down after smearing a little water along the edges.
  8. Prepare the batter by adding all the ingredients into the Besan and beating it for about 5-7 minutes. Add Soda and beat again to get a light batter.
  9. Dip the stuffed slices into the Batter and lower into a kadai of hot oil. Fry well on both sides. Drain well and take out.
  10. Cut it into two halves while hot. Enjoy with tomato sauce.
