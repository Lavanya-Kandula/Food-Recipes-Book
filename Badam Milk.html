Badam Milk

Badam Milk or Almond shake is usually a comforting and nutritious beverage made out of Badam (Almonds) and milk, a fantastic wholesome and nutritious beverage for children.

Ingredients

  • Sugar - half cup
  • Milk - 5 cups
  • Almonds(badam) - half cup
  • Cashewnuts(chopped) - 2 teaspoons
  • Rose essence - 2 drops
  • Saffron - pinch
  • Mint leaves(pudhina) - 1 tablespoon

Preparation

  1. Boil the almonds in a pan for 5 minutes. Remove and drain.
  2. Peel the skin off the almonds and grind in a blender to a smooth paste. Keep it aside.
  3. Dissolve the saffron in 2 tablespoons of warm milk and keep it aside too.
  4. Boil the milk in a pan then add the almond paste and mix thoroughly. Simmer the milk over a low flame for 3 minutes.
  5. Remove from the flame and strain the milk. Allow the milk to cool.
  6. Add the dissolved saffron and the rose essence and mix thoroughly.
  7. Sprinkle the chopped cashew nuts and the mint leaves on top of the milk.
