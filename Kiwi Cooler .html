Kiwi Coloer

This Kiwi Cooler is an ultra refreshing drink for summer or for parties.

Ingredients

  • 2 Kiwi
  • 3 Thinly sliced Lemons
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • Sprite/Cool Soda
  • 7 - 8 Ice Cubes

Preparation

  1. Crush Kiwi pieces, thinly sliced Lemons and Ice in a mortar or a tall glass.
  2. After the mixture is crushed more than a half, add Sugar and crush to 80%
  3. Fill a glass to 1/3rd with Kiwi Crush and add Ice Cubes. Pour Sprite over it to fill the glass.
  4. Mix from the bottom with a spoon.
