Dal Khichidi Recipe

It is always a pleasure to make or eat soothing 'Dal Khichdi'. It is very easy to make.In India people make different varieties of Khichdi.The recipe I am giving you is less spicy and delicious .

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • Green gram
  • 3/4 cup Red gram
  • 1 Potato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 cup Fresh peas
  • 3 Green chilli
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • Salt-As per taste
  • 8 cups Water
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • For Seasoning
  • 4 tbsp Ghee
  • 2 Pinches Asafoetida
  • 3 Red chilli
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 2 Tomato pieces
  • 1 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  • 1 Bunch Coriander leaves

Preparation

  1. Add some Oil to a cooker and fry chopped onions till they turn golden brown.
  2. Add Ginger Garlic paste and slit Green Chillies and fry on a low flame.
  3. Now add Potato pieces, soaked Green Gram, Red Gram, Rice, Turmeric, Salt, and fry till you remove the moisture on a medium flame.
  4. Add 1/2 cup Green Peas, 6 cups water and cook them on a medium flame.
  5. Once it is cooked well, add 2 more cups of water and allow it to steam for some time.
  6. Take some Ghee for seasoning. Add Asafoetida, Red Chillies, Cumin, and Tomatoes. Fry till the tomatoes become
  7. Switch off the stove, add Chilli powder to the seasoning and add it to the Khichdi. Garnish it with chopped Coriander leaves and serve the 'Dal Khichdi' hot with a Papad.
