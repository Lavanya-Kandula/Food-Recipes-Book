Rayalaseema Mutton Fry

Mutton Fry is made in all areas, but the type made in Rayalaseema is spicy and strong. And really appeals to those who like very spicy food. This is especially made in the districts of Kurnool and Chittoor. It stays fresh for 2-3 days and goes well with Sambar or Rasam.
