Pomegranate Drink

Pomegranate is one of the best cocktail ingredients we know, offering tartness and tang to balance a drink's sweetness, deep flavor that stands up to booze, and even a little tannic texture to make your drink more interesting.

Ingredients

  • Pomegranate - 1 no
  • Mint leaves - 10 leaf
  • Ice cubes - 3-4 numbers
  • Lime juice - 1+1/2 tea spoon
  • Sugar syrup - 1 teaspoons
  • Soda - 1 cup

Preparation

  1. Take a blender, add pomegranate and blend it to extract juice transfer into a bowl.
  2. Crush mint leaves, pomegranate with a pestle.
  3. Take a serving glass add pomegranate juice, the crush of pomegranate, mint leaves, ice cubes, pomegranate, lime juice, sugar syrup, soda and mix slightly.
  4. Now refreshing Pomegranate drink is ready to serve.
