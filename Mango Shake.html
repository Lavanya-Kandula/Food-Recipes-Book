Mango Shake

Mango shake is a luscious and refreshing beverage which is made with sweet ripe mangoes blended with little sugar and milk.

Ingredients

  • Mango - 2 numbers
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoons
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Ice cube - 1 tablespoon
  • Yellow food color (optional) - 1/2 tea spoon
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional) - 1 tea spoon

Preparation

  1. Blend the mango cubes, sugar, ice cubes in a blender or mixer in a nice puree.
  2. You can add yellow colour for getting a nice colour. (Optional)
  3. Add milk to the mango pulp and blend well once again.
  4. Pour the shake into tall glasses.
  5. You can also add a scoop of vanilla ice cream before serving. (Optional)
