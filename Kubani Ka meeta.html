Kubani Ka meeta

Khubani/ Qubani Ka Meetha is another famous Hyderabadi sweet dish made of dried Apricots & nuts. It is a popular at Hyderabadi feasts and weddings.

Ingredients

  • Water - 1 cup
  • Sugar - to taste
  • Apricots - 20 number

Preparation

  1. Soak apricots in water for over night.Seprate seeds from apricots and keep it a side.Break the seeds will find nuts ( almonds) keep it a side.
  2. Now take a pan add apricots and water let it boil for 15 minutes by closing with a lid.
  3. After 15 minutes remove the lid mash apricots with masher or spoon then add sugar as per taste then nuts let it cook for another 5-10 minutes .
  4. After 10 minutes transfer it in a bowl let it cool down can serve with ice cream or curstad,fresh cream.
